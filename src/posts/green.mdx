In 2023, seeing as 2.1 was coming to a close, I started making [VERTICAL](/blog/vertical) after seeing that I hadn't
actually released any major levels after [Side Project](https://youtu.be/G6X8EqkwQEk).
I wanted to make something as fun as possible, especially after seeing that Side Project
was a lot more of a pain to play than I expected.

I wanted to make a level that felt like my 2.1 magnum opus in as many aspects as I could, and I think I can say
I did that job quite well, as I still feel quite proud about the level and
people are [still calling it fun](https://gdbrowser.com/comments/96902741) despite the level
being two and a half years old at this point.

---

After 2.2 came out, the first thing I was certain of was that no new level of mine was gonna
be worked on for a long time. I indeed did use all my artistic creativity on VERTICAL, and
I had in mind that years would pass until I get the creativity needed to do something new.

But time was happening, and the years came and went. Next time I check and VERTICAL turned
two years old.

It's a bit of an open secret right now that all the green shitpost was hinting at a new level being made.

In March, I felt the need of doing something about this, just working on a good level with the purpose of
releasing it quick, as a 2.2 gameplay experiment. I was aware it wouldn't be anywhere as remarkable as
VERTICAL, but I still wanted to come back to creating.

So the days passed, I aimed at a release on April 1st (which would have given me less than two weeks
to make the level from start to finish), and after seeing that there's no way to make a good level that quickly,
I pushed the date back to later that month.

After reviewing the level again, I don't think I can say I am proud of how the level is turning out.
I could fix the gameplay and take my time to polish and finish everything, but that would take a long
time and it still wouldn't get close to VERTICAL in terms of quality and originality.

So I have made the decision to cancel the level.

---

Not all hope is lost, though. Working on this level has taught me many things about making
levels in 2.2, and there were a few parts I built that I found quite good!

I believe I can make a very good level if I let myself take as long as I need, and in a context
of enjoying the creation process as much as I can.

---

And speaking of canceled levels, holy shit I have so many abandoned projects!

showcase **every single abandoned level I've ever made**. Yes, even that one.
Stay tuned for the premiere next week.

Until we meet again.
